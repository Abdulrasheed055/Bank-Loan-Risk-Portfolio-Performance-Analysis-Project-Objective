#  Bank Loan Risk & Portfolio Performance Analysis

## Project Objective

This project analyzes a bank loan dataset to evaluate portfolio performance, measure credit risk exposure, and identify key drivers of loan default.

The analysis is based on **38,600+ loan applications** and simulates real-world financial analytics used by lending institutions to support **risk management and strategic decision-making**.

---

##  Key Portfolio Metrics

*  Total Loan Applications: **38.6K**
*  Total Funded Amount: **$435.8M**
*  Total Amount Received: **$473.1M**
*  Average Interest Rate: **12.05%**
*  Average Debt-to-Income (DTI): **13.33%**

---

## 🔍 Business Insights & Performance Analysis

### Loan Quality Segmentation

* **86.18%** of loans are classified as **Good Loans**
* **13.82%** are classified as **Bad Loans (Default/Charged-Off)**
  ➡️ Indicates a generally strong portfolio, but with measurable credit risk exposure

---

### Loan Term Risk Analysis

* **60-month loans** contribute a larger share of total risk exposure
* Longer-term loans show **higher default likelihood** compared to **36-month loans**
  ➡️ Loan duration is a key driver of credit risk

---

###  Portfolio Profitability Insight

* Total Amount Received (**$473.1M**) exceeds Total Funded (**$435.8M**)
  ➡️ Indicates **positive portfolio returns and profitability performance**

---

###  Borrower Employment Risk

* Borrowers with **10+ years employment** show **strong repayment behavior**
* Borrowers with **<1 year employment** show **higher default tendencies**
  ➡️ Employment stability is a critical factor in credit risk assessment

---

###  Home Ownership Analysis

* **RENT category** has the highest loan application volume
* **Mortgage holders** demonstrate **more stable repayment patterns**
  ➡️ Housing status influences borrower reliability

---

###  Interest Rate & Risk Relationship

* **Charged-off loans** have higher average interest rates than fully paid loans
* Higher interest rates correlate with **higher credit risk grading**
  ➡️ Pricing reflects borrower risk profile

---

###  Loan Purpose Distribution

Top loan application categories:

* Credit Card
* Debt Consolidation
* Home Improvement
* Small Business

➡️ Indicates key demand areas for lending products

---

##  Dashboard Features

* Loan portfolio performance tracking (Funded vs Received)
* Credit risk segmentation (Good vs Bad loans)
* Loan term analysis (36 vs 60 months)
* Interest rate and DTI monitoring
* Borrower segmentation (employment length, home ownership)
* Loan purpose and category analysis

## 📌 Strategic Recommendations

* Apply stricter credit assessment for **long-term (60-month) loans**
* Monitor borrowers with **high DTI ratios** more closely
* Implement **risk-based pricing models** across loan grades
* Encourage **shorter-term loan structures** to reduce default exposure
* Develop **early warning systems** for high-risk borrower segments
